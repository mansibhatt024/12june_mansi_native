Q=4 MF Cod Rule of RDBMS Systems:
           -There isn't a commonly known term "MF Cod Rule" in the context of RDBMS. 
           -It's possible that you meant something else or there might be a typo in the term.
           -Codd's rule in DBMS also known as Codd's 12 rules/commandments is a set of thirteen rules (numbered 0 to 12) that define a 
            database to be a correct Relational Database Management System (RDBMS). 
           -If a database follows Codd's 12 rules, it is called a True relational database management system.
